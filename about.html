About

Thank you for visiting my website I am grateful that you stopped by!

It has been my lifelong passion to be a great musician. I grew up in the small village of Swannanoa, North Carolina. I have 3 siblings, 2 sisters Peggy and Marsha and 1 brother Jonathan. I have been in Nashville TN making a living in the music industry for over 37 years and have had a very blessed career. I am involved in many aspects of the business and enjoy sharing knowledge from my many years of grinding it out in Music City. I currently serve as the touring bassist for The Artimus Pyle Band (Lynyrd Skynyrd).

Please reach out with any questions .. things I am able to help with include but are not limited to tour managing, reading Nashville number charts, producing your song demos and making custom records and much, much more.
